# 2025 Plan Benefits Summary

Document reference: BEN-2025
Effective date: 1 January 2025
Applies to SilverCare PPO and GoldCare HMO. All amounts are per member, per plan year.

## Deductibles and out-of-pocket maximums

| | SilverCare PPO | GoldCare HMO |
|---|---|---|
| Individual deductible | $2,500 | $1,000 |
| Family deductible | $5,000 | $2,000 |
| Individual out-of-pocket maximum | $8,150 | $6,400 |
| Family out-of-pocket maximum | $16,300 | $12,800 |
| Monthly premium (individual) | $412 | $536 |
| Monthly premium (family) | $1,180 | $1,505 |

GoldCare HMO carries the lower deductible but the higher monthly premium. SilverCare PPO
members pay more before coverage begins and less each month. The deductible does not apply to
preventive services under either plan.

## Pharmacy copays by formulary tier

| Tier | Description | SilverCare PPO | GoldCare HMO |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | Generic | $10 | $5 |
| Tier 2 | Preferred generic / biosimilar | $25 | $20 |
| Tier 3 | Preferred brand | $60 | $45 |
| Tier 4 | Specialty | $250 | 20% coinsurance |

Tier 4 specialty medications are subject to coinsurance rather than a flat copay under
GoldCare HMO. Specialty medications must be filled through the designated specialty pharmacy
network.

Mail-order fulfilment provides a 90-day supply for two copays at Tiers 1 through 3. Tier 4
medications are not eligible for mail order and are limited to 30-day supplies.

## Medical service cost sharing

| Service | SilverCare PPO | GoldCare HMO |
|---|---|---|
| Primary care visit | $30 | $20 |
| Specialist visit | $65 | $40 |
| MRI or CT scan | $400 after deductible | $250 after deductible |
| Emergency room visit | $500 | $350 |
| Urgent care visit | $75 | $50 |
| Outpatient surgery | 20% after deductible | 15% after deductible |
| Inpatient hospital stay | 20% after deductible | 15% after deductible |
| Physical therapy (per visit) | $45 | $30 |

The emergency room copay is waived if the visit results in an inpatient admission.

## Annual visit limits

| Service | Limit (both plans) |
|---|---|
| Physical therapy | 30 visits |
| Occupational therapy | 20 visits |
| Chiropractic care | 12 visits |
| Nutritional counselling | 6 visits |

Visits beyond these limits require prior authorization and documentation of continued
functional improvement.

## Network rules

SilverCare PPO covers out-of-network care at 60 percent of allowed charges after the
out-of-network deductible of $5,000. GoldCare HMO does not cover out-of-network care except for
emergency services and for approved continuity-of-care arrangements.

Referrals are required for specialist visits under GoldCare HMO. SilverCare PPO does not
require referrals.

## Preventive services

Preventive services are covered at 100 percent with no cost sharing under both plans when
delivered in network. This includes annual wellness visits, routine immunisations, and
guideline-recommended cancer screenings.

A service billed as diagnostic rather than preventive is subject to the applicable cost
sharing, even when performed during a preventive visit.

## Coordination with pharmacy policy

Formulary tier assignments are maintained in the individual drug policies, not in this
document. Zeltavir is assigned to Tier 4 and Morvanex to Tier 3; consult PA-2291 and PA-2304
respectively for the authorization criteria that apply to each.
